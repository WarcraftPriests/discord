# Talent Builds
With current tuning in pre-patch :archon~1: Archon and :voidweaver: Voidweaver are both perfectly viable in all forms of content. Differences between the two are marginal.

_Note_: the following builds have specifically been optimized for pre-patch. Build variety and viability will be different once we get access to Apex Talents in Midnight.
