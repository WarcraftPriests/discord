## Gems
- Epic Gem: [Indecipherable Eversong Diamond](https://www.wowhead.com/beta/item=240983)
- Rare Gem: [Flawless Masterful Peridot](https://www.wowhead.com/item=240892) or [Flawless Quick Amethyst](https://www.wowhead.com/item=240900)
Rare gems are roughly worth 0.4% DPS. If you are considering replacing an item with a socket, keep this in mind. As you get more gear, this could be even higher, but this is a rough number you can use. Simming different gem choices is extremely difficult because of how low of an increase this is, you should take gem sims with a large amount of caution as, typically, gains that are that small when comparing gems are within the margin of error.
