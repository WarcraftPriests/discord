# 🐉 Chimaerus, the Undreamt God
## TL;DR
- 🟢 **Consuming Miasma** – dispel players inside green zones to clear space
- 💥 **Caustic Phlegm** – ticking curse damage
- 🐛 **Adds spawn during flight** – kill them before boss lands
- 🦅 **Ravenous Dive** – boss lands → huge raid damage
- ❗ Any adds alive during Dive = boss heals & raid takes massive damage
## Roles
**Tanks**
- 📍 Face boss away (frontal)
- 🐛 Keep adds from reaching boss
**Healers**
- 💥 Heavy damage on **Ravenous Dive**
- 🟢 Dispel **Consuming Miasma** in safe zones
**DPS**
- 🎯 Kill **adds immediately**
- 🧠 Do NOT waste cooldowns while boss is flying
## Assignments
- 🐛 **Add priority:** kill before Dive
- 🛡️ **Raid CDs:** use on boss landing
- 🚀 **Lust:** After first landing
