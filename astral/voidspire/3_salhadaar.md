# 🐉 Fallen-King Salhadaar
## TL;DR
- 🟣 **Orbs (Void Convergence)** – kill before they reach boss or raid wipes
- 💥 **Orb explosions** give **Dark Radiation** → wait before killing 2nd orb
- ➡️ **Shattering Twilight** – arrows point spike direction (aim away from raid/boss)
- ⭕ **Despotic Command** – drop puddles at edge
- 🧊 **Fractured Images** – CC/stop casts or they leave permanent puddles
- 🔥 **Entropic Unraveling (100 energy)** – beams spin → dodge + big DPS window
## Roles
**Tanks**
- 🔁 Swap **Destabilizing Strikes** stacks
- 📍 Drag boss toward orb gates while kiting
**Healers**
- 💥 **Dark Radiation** orb explosions hurt the raid
- 💥 Heavy damage during **Entropic Unraveling**
**DPS**
- 🎯 Kill **orbs immediately**
- 🧊 Stop **Fractured Image casts** (any CC works)
- 🧠 Wait for **Dark Radiation** to drop before killing next orb
## Assignments
- 🚀 **Lust:** Pull or during **Entropic Unraveling**
- 🟣 **Orb plan:** kill 1 → wait for debuff → kill 2
- 📍 **Puddles:** drop at edge to keep center clean
