# :dragon: Vaelgor & Ezzorak
## TL;DR
- :crossed_swords: **Council fight** – bosses do NOT share health
- :purple_circle: **Void pools** – drop in center, loose stack
- :link: Break tether when active (Tank keeps theirs until the end)
- :purple_circle: Soak Large Purple Void Orb until it glows White (check debuff)
## Roles
**Tanks**
- :repeat: Swap bosses when debuffs stack
- :round_pushpin: Keep bosses separated, raid should not be in front or behind the boss
**Healers**
- :purple_circle: Watch players soaking orbs
- :magic_wand: Get ready to dispel player with Dread Breath
- :herodamage: Watch stacks of **Midnight Manifestation** on players
**DPS**
- :zap: Kick dangerous casts
- :brain: Handle movement mechanics cleanly
- :heart: Split Boss damage so they die at the same time.
- :stanSTARE: Focus Manifestation of Midnight when spawned
## Assignments
- :rocket: **Lust:** On-Pull
- :round_pushpin: **Puddles:** Drop in center
