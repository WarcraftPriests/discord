# 🐉 Crown of the Cosmos
## TL;DR
- 🌌 **Multi-phase fight** with increasing raid damage
- 🌀 Dodge cosmic swirlies / beams
- 👥 Soak required mechanics when called
- ⚡ Interrupt dangerous casts
- 💥 Final phase = burn
## Roles
**Tanks**
- 🔁 Swap tank debuffs
- 📍 Move boss for mechanics
**Healers**
- 💥 Damage ramps each phase
- 💥 Prepare cooldowns for final burn
**DPS**
- 🎯 Kill priority adds first
- ⚡ Interrupt dangerous casts
- 🔥 Push boss before mechanics overwhelm raid
## Assignments
- 🚀 **Lust:** Final phase burn
- 👥 **Soaks:** Assigned if needed
