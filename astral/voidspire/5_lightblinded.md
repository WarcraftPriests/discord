# 🐉 Lightblinded Vanguard
## TL;DR
- 👥 **Soak light zones** to prevent raid damage
- 💥 **Radiant bursts** cause heavy raid damage
- 🌀 Dodge frontal beams and swirlies
- ⚡ Interrupt dangerous add casts
- 🧠 Manage positioning so raid isn’t blinded
## Roles
**Tanks**
- 🔁 Swap tank debuff stacks
- 📍 Position boss away from raid
**Healers**
- 💥 Burst healing during **Radiant damage windows**
- 👥 Players soaking may need extra healing
**DPS**
- 🎯 Kill adds quickly
- ⚡ Interrupt dangerous casts
- 🌀 Dodge beams / swirlies
## Assignments
- 👥 **Soak groups:** rotate if needed
- 🚀 **Lust:** During burn phase
