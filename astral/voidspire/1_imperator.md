# :dragon: Imperator Averzian
## TL;DR
- :busts_in_silhouette: **Umbral Collapse** – soak 2 of the 3 Voidshaper tiles
- :brain: **Board mechanic** – prevent boss from claiming 3 tiles in a row
- :zap: **Kick Pitch Bulwark** (add shield cast)
- :o: **Oblivion circles** – 1 player per circle
- :cyclone: Dodge swirlies / beams
## Roles
**Tanks**
- :repeat: Swap at ~8–10 **Blackening Wounds**
- :round_pushpin: Keep boss away from claimed tiles
**Healers**
- :boom: Big damage during **Collapse soaks**
- :boom: Damage ramps as more tiles get claimed
**DPS**
- :dart: Kill priority: **Voidshaper → other adds → boss**
- :zap: Kick **Pitch Bulwark**
## Assignments
